Shipping Policy

At Fresh Dry Fruits, we strive to deliver your orders in a timely and safe manner. Please read our shipping policy carefully.

1. Shipping Locations

We currently ship across India. Delivery availability may vary based on location and courier serviceability.

2. Order Processing Time

Orders are usually processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.

3. Delivery Timeline

Estimated delivery time ranges from 3–7 business days, depending on your location. Delays may occur due to weather, courier issues, or festive seasons.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be clearly mentioned at the time of order. Bulk or special orders may have different shipping costs.

5. Delivery Partners

We use trusted third-party courier partners to ensure safe delivery of your orders.

6. Incorrect Address

Please ensure that the shipping address provided is accurate. Fresh Dry Fruits will not be responsible for delays or losses caused by incorrect addresses.

7. Damaged or Missing Items

If you receive a damaged package or missing items, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with supporting images.
